Travel opportunities

Currently, only Moon, Mars, and Mercury are available for travel. Neptune is estimated to be visitable within a few decades.

The Moon

Visit the moon, earth's one and only natural satellite. Take trail walks of nearly 12,000 miles, stays are available for up to 6 days!

Mars

Travel to the 4th planet in our solar system. Our destination Mars travel package includes a 3-day stay and a 2,000-mile trail walk.

Mercury

During your selected stay at Mercury, you will be able to stay up to 3 minutes. Outdoors, we have about 3 miles of trail walks.

Neptune

Neptune is our most anticipated travel destination by our clients. As this is the furthest planet, scientists estimate the travel waiting time is two decades.
