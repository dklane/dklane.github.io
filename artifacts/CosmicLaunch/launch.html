Precautions of
Launch

See how we keep our astronauts safe during their travel journeys. All of our customers are guaranteed a safe journey to their outer space experiences.

Training

In order to make sure our future astronauts will be safe not only to launch but also during their stay, training is a requirement for all. Training time is about 2 weeks of physical strength, diet, suit control, and low-gravity walks. During physical training, trainees will be required to go to the gym for at least 6 hours. The diet will consist of only dry food to prepare trainees for the experience they will have during their stay. Suit control and low-gravity walks will is a requirement to attend after physical training. Trainees are required to train at least 4 of the 2 weeks of experience.

Gear

Cosmic Launch provides the best quality space gear. All suits are created with pure steel, and cotton, tinfoil for gloves, as well as leather boots for the best footprints on outer space rocks.

Rockets

Our Rockets were originally planned 200 years ago, which resulted in the best protection for astronauts today. With the outer-layer made of pure silver and the interior of silk, feel comfort while launching!

Recovery

During recovery, people will be placed from the rocket they used to reach outer space to another called "Bacq Home". The rocket "Bacq Home" will be launched every month approximately 3 miles from the nearest landing area on each planet.
