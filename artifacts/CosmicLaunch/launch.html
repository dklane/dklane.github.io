Precautions of
Launch

See how we keep our astronauts safe during their travel journeys.

Training

In order to make sure our future astronauts will be safe in not only launch, but also during their stay, training is a requirement for all. Training time is about 2 weeks of physical strength, diet, suit control, and low gravity walks.

Gear

Cosmic Launch provides the best quality space gear. All suits are created with pure steel, and cotton, tinfoil for gloves, as well as leather boots for the best footprints on outer space rocks.

Rockets

Our Rockets were originally planed 200 years ago, which resulted in the best protection for astronauts today. With the outer-layer made of pure silver and interior of silk, feel comfort while launching!
