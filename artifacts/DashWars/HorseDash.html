Meetings

Due to the pandemic, this competition will be a virtual event. This section of the website will help Students who are interested in participating in the competition with more instruction on how to compete virtually!

Zoom Call Vectors by Vecteezy

Competitors

Signing up

Participants must sign up on the join page before the sign-up window closes. Once students are signed up, they will be sent an email with a link that will help them choose a meeting time in Zoom.

Zoom

The competition will be hosted in a Zoom call. Zoom is an online-based application that allows one-on-one video chats to group meetings.Click here to learn more about Zoom!

Sessions

During the Zoom meeting, participants are expected to have their cameras turned on, in a setting with lighting. They must have their "share screen" or broadcasts on. These terms will help conform to the correct player and the gameplay. Constants will be timed in their gameplay by Judges. The timer will start immediately after constant moves their sprite.

Awards Ceremony

Winners (1st, 2nd, and 3rd) will be announced in a YouTube live video. This video will be available in the email sent to participants with the Zoom sign-up link on May 22nd.
