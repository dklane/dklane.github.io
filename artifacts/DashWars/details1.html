Game Functionality & Meeting Sessions


Meeting Vectors by Vecteezy

Game

The game Horse Dash should be playable on desktop computers, laptops, Chromebooks, and others. However, this game is not functional on mobile or tablet devices that do not operate with a keyboard.

Device

Please make sure to have a device available before the Zoom session and the game ready to be played, this will help insure that our judges can join sessions promptly with assigned contestants.

Errors

If there are any errors with Zoom sessions, Game functionality, or others, please contact our email DashWars@outlook.com. We will try our best to respond within 3 business days.
