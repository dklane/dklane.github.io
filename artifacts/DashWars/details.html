Details

Players must be registered in the join page in order to receive an email containing a direct link to the call where they will be judged live. The deadline for registering will be May 1st, 2022.

Eligibility

Children only of or below the ages of 12 will be eligible to compete in the virtual event.

Where

The competition will be hosted in separate Zoom call sessions. Players will be in a one-on-one call with their judges. Check the rules page for more information.

When

Players will have individual one-on-one zoom call times presented to them. These Zoom sessions will be from May 17th, 2022 through May 19, 2022. Zoom calls will be assigned through selected times and dates in an email sent to registered users only on May 2nd, 2022. The form must be completed within the 1-week window.

How will contestants be judged?

Participants will be judged by their timing on how fast they complete the game to the certification layout of the game. If the participant's horse (sprite) collides with any eliminating blocks (Yellow, or the ground) that restart the layout, the participant will be eliminated.
